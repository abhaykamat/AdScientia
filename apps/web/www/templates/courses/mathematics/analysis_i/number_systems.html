Real Numbers Axioms
  1. $\forall x, y \in \mathbb{R},\hspace{4 mm} x+y \in \mathbb{R}$ and $xy \in \mathbb{R}$.
  2. $\forall x, y, z \in \mathbb{R}:\hspace{4 mm} (x+y)+z = x+(y+z)$.
  3. $\forall x,y \in \mathbb{R}:\hspace{4 mm} x+y = y+x$.
  4. $\exists 0 \in \mathbb{R}$ s.t. $\hspace{4 mm} x+0=0+x=x\hspace{4 mm}\forall x \in \mathbb{R}$.
  5. $\forall x \in \mathbb{R}, \exists (-x) \in \mathbb{R}$ s.t. $\hspace{4 mm} x+ (-x) = (-x) + x = 0$.
  6. $\forall x,y,z \in \mathbb{R},\hspace{4 mm}(xy)z=x(yz)$.
  7. $\forall x,y \in \mathbb{R},\hspace{4 mm}xy=yx$.
  8. $\exists 1 \in \mathbb{R}$ s.t. $\hspace{4 mm}x \cdot 1 = 1 \cdot x,\hspace{4 mm} \forall x \in \mathbb{R}$.
  9. $\forall x \in \mathbb{R}$ s.t. $x \neq 0, \exists (x^{-1}) \in \mathbb{R}$ s.t. $\hspace{4 mm} x(x^{-1}) = (x^{-1})x = 1$.
  10. $\forall x,y,z \in \mathbb{R}\hspace{4 mm} x(y+z)=xy+xz$.
Order Relations Axions
  1. $\forall x,y \in \mathbb{R},$ either $x<y$, $x=y$ or $y<x$.
  2. If $x<y$ and $y<z$ then $x<z$.
  3. If $x<y$ and $z>0$ then $xz < yz$.
