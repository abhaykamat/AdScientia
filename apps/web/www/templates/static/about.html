About AdScientia.org

Open-source scientific degree programmes available to everybody

AdScientia's goal is to provide top-tier undergraduate and postgraduate level scientific degree resources to anybody who has the desire to learn.

Our aim is to make our content, our software and our process free to use and open to improvement. We achieve this without biases or sacrifices in content quality.
