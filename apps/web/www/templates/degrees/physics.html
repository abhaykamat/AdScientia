Physics

Four Year Undergraduate Degree Programme - MSc/MPhys

Physics, as a subject, concerns itself with understanding the world around us. In particular, it attempts to produce models for observed phenomena, which can then be used to make predictions that are subsequently tested to refine the models. The AdScientia Physics degree course provides resources equivalent to a top-tier university masters level education.
